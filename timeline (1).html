My adventure timeline

Civil engineer turned into product designer. I am passionate about transforming early stage ideas into impactful solutions for both startups and enterprise clients.

2015-19 : Engineering my future

Let's just say I spent these years mastering the art of building things – not just houses and skyscrapers (thanks Civil Engineering!), but also a solid foundation for my future career.

2019-20 : Taking a Digital Leap

Ever heard of a civil engineer turning digital marketer? Well, that was me! I traded in blueprints for marketing strategies and dived headfirst into the exciting world of making things visible online. Worked for a Singaporean company remotely from India.

2020-21 : From Marketer to Maker

The digital world was cool, but a creative itch started nagging at me. Enter the fascinating world of User Experience (UX) Design! I co-founded "Urban Tribe," a print-on-demand e-commerce website, where I got to combine my newfound design skills with my love for making things (this time, awesome t-shirts!).

2021-22 : Luxury, Design, and Travel

Bonjour, France! I moved to the beautiful city of Grenoble to pursue a Master's degree in Luxury Brand Management.While soaking up all things luxury, I also kept my design skills sharp by working as a product designer for a remote event management startup called "The Plannerss."I also landed a UX design and research internship at Amadeus, a leading travel IT company. A perfect blend of my interests – design, travel, and a touch of luxury!

2023- present : Building User Experiences at Amadeus

And here I am today, a full-fledged UX designer at Amadeus, making sure your travel experiences are as smooth and delightful as possible! This timeline is a glimpse into my journey – always learning, exploring different paths, and building something new along the way
